Formula and scouting scales

Opponent defense score = teleop EPA × 0.60 + contribution % × weight + vulnerability × 5.

Recommended defender fit = defense ability × 10 - teleop EPA × 0.60 - endgame EPA × 0.40.

Vulnerability: 5 = easy to slow down, 3 = average, 1 = hard to affect. Endgame disruptability: 5 = likely can delay/stop, 1 = leave them alone. Defense ability: 5 = strong defender, 1 = poor defender.
